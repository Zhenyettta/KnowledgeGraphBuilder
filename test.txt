Elon Musk, the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, was born in Pretoria, South Africa, on June 28, 1971. He moved to the United States in 2002 and became a naturalized American citizen. Musk is widely recognized for his contributions to electric vehicles and space exploration. He founded SpaceX in 2002 to revolutionize space travel. SpaceX launched its first successful mission in 2008, and later, the company began to work closely with NASA. In addition to SpaceX, Musk also founded Tesla in 2003, a company focused on electric vehicles and sustainable energy solutions. In 2021, Musk became the richest person in the world according to Forbes magazine.
